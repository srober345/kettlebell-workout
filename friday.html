Power Day

Friday Workout

Goal: Maximum force per rep — heavier bells, explosive intent
Pace: Deliberate — HR to 130-135 bpm (~45-60 sec rest)
Time: 60-70 minutes
# Exercise Sets x Reps Bell Notes Demo
1 Kettlebell Swings 10 x 10 HEAVIEST Maximum hip snap. Squeeze glutes HARD at top. Every rep like it's the only one. Watch Demo
2 High Pulls 10 x 10 Heavy Aggressive pull. Elbows should fly up. Power from the hips. Watch Demo
3 Side (Golf) Swings 10 x 10 Heavy Explosive rotation. Feel the core and obliques drive the movement. Watch Demo
4 Rows 5 x 20 HEAVIEST Slow, heavy pulls. 2-second hold at the top. Feel the lats engage fully. Watch Demo
5 Push Outs 5 x 20 Heavy Push with intent. Full lockout. Controlled 2-second return. Form Cues
6 Overhead Press 5 x 20 Heavy Strongest pressing effort of the week. Lock out fully. Watch Demo
7 Low Chest Flys 5 x 20 Heavy Heavier bell, controlled arc. Don't swing — lift deliberately. Form Cues
8 Bicep Curls + Press 5 x 20 Medium Step up the weight. Full curl, full press, full lockout. Form Cues
9 Tricep Extensions 5 x 20 Medium Heavier than Wednesday. Slow down (2 sec), explosive press up. Form Cues
10 Side Extensions 5 x 20 Heavy Heavier bell. Slow controlled lean. Feel every inch of the stretch. Watch Demo
11 Waist Halos 4 x 25 Medium Step up from light to medium. More core engagement. Watch Demo
12 Head Halos 4 x 25 Medium Heavier halo for more trap and shoulder demand. Form Cues
Total: 1,200 reps — Heavier loads = greater strength and muscle-toning stimulus

Cool-Down (5 Minutes)

  • Walk slowly for 2 minutes
  • Full-body stretch: hip flexors, hamstrings, chest, lats — 30 sec each
  • Shoulder stretch: 30 sec per side
