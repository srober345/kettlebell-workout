How They Work Together

1

No Overlap

Kettlebell days train full-body strength and conditioning. Ab days target core muscles directly. Never the same muscle groups two days in a row.

2

Complementary Training

Kettlebell swings and presses build core stability indirectly. Dedicated ab work builds the visible strength and definition that compound movements alone can't achieve.

3

Built-In Recovery

Sunday rest. Ab sessions are 15-20 minutes — short enough that they serve as active recovery between heavy kettlebell days.
