Hypertrophy Day

Hypertrophy Workout

Goal: Mechanical tension + metabolic stress — maximum muscle growth
Rest: 60-75 sec between supersets
Tempo: 2-1-1 (2s down, 1s pause, 1s up)
Time: ~50 minutes

Agonist-antagonist supersets — perform 1a then 1b with no rest between, then rest before next superset.

# Exercise Sets x Reps Weight Rest Notes Demo
1a SS1Goblet Squat 3 x 12 25-30 lb — Hold one DB at chest. Full depth — deeper = more glute activation. Pause 1 sec at bottom. Form Cues
1b SS1DB Romanian Deadlift 3 x 12 25-30 lb 75s Hinge pattern — opposite of squat. Pause 1 sec at full stretch before driving up. Form Cues
2a SS2Flat DB Press 3 x 12 20-25 lb — Full ROM. 1-sec pause at chest stretch. Press to lockout, squeeze chest. Form Cues
2b SS2One-Arm Row 3 x 12/arm 20-25 lb 60s Pull to hip. Squeeze 1 sec. 12 reps per arm = push/pull balance. Form Cues
3a SS3DB Floor Fly 3 x 12 15-20 lb — Lying on floor. Lower to floor touch, squeeze back up. Floor limits ROM for safety. Form Cues
3b SS3Standing OH Press 3 x 10 15-20 lb 60s After chest work, shoulders are warm. Press to full lockout. Core stays tight. Form Cues
4a SS4Hammer Curl 3 x 12 15-20 lb — Neutral grip. No swinging. 2-sec negative. Squeeze at top. Form Cues
4b SS4DB Skull Crusher 3 x 12 15-20 lb 60s Lower DBs to temples slowly (3 sec). Elbows stay fixed. Full extension at top. Form Cues
5a SS5Lateral Raise 3 x 12 10-15 lb — Slight forward lean. Raise to shoulder height only — higher adds impingement risk. Form Cues
5b SS5Bent-Over Rear Delt Fly 3 x 12 10-15 lb 60s Hinged 45°. Fly motion. Squeeze shoulder blades 1 sec at top. Control down. Form Cues
6 Reverse Lunge 3 x 10/leg 20-25 lb 60s Step back, not forward — less knee shear. Drop rear knee toward floor. Drive through front heel. Form Cues
Total: ~340 reps — 33 working sets — 5 supersets + 1 straight set
