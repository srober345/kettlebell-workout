Standard Day

Monday Workout

Goal: Balanced baseline — solid effort across all 12 exercises
Pace: Steady — HR to 140-145 bpm between sets (~30 sec)
Time: 55-65 minutes
# Exercise Sets x Reps Bell Notes Demo
1 Kettlebell Swings 10 x 10 Heavy Explosive hip snap every rep. 10x10 keeps each set short enough to maintain full power. Watch Demo
2 High Pulls 10 x 10 Medium Pull to chin height, elbows high and wide. Short explosive sets. Drive with hips, not arms. Watch Demo
3 Side (Golf) Swings 5 x 20 Medium Controlled rotation through core. 10 each direction per set. Watch Demo
4 Rows 5 x 20 Heavy 10 per arm per set. Squeeze shoulder blade at top for 1 second. Control the lowering. Watch Demo
5 Push Outs 5 x 20 Medium Start at chest, press horizontally. Full arm extension. Controlled return. Form Cues
6 Overhead Press 5 x 20 Medium Start at chest, press straight up to full lockout. 10 per arm or 20 two-handed. Watch Demo
7 Low Chest Flys 4 x 25 Medium Arms at sides, raise to chest height in a forward arc. Controlled — don't use momentum. Form Cues
8 Bicep Curls + Press 4 x 25 Light-Med One fluid motion: curl to shoulder, press to sky. 12-13 per arm per set. Form Cues
9 Tricep Extensions 4 x 25 Light-Med Elbows tight to head, full stretch behind head, full lockout at top. Form Cues
10 Side Extensions 4 x 25 Medium 12-13 per side per set. Lean slowly, feel the oblique stretch. Watch Demo
11 Waist Halos 4 x 25 Light Circles around the waist. Alternate direction each set. Watch Demo
12 Head Halos 4 x 25 Light Circles around the head. Alternate direction each set. Slow and controlled. Form Cues
Total: 1,200 reps — 12 exercises x 100 reps each

Cool-Down (5 Minutes)

  • Walk slowly for 2 minutes
  • Hip flexor stretch: 30 sec per side
  • Chest doorway stretch: 30 sec per side
  • Hamstring stretch (standing or seated): 30 sec per side
