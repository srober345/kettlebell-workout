Density Day

Wednesday Workout

Goal: Maximum metabolic burn — faster pace, shorter rest
Pace: Aggressive — 15-20 sec rest, let HR stay at 150-155
Time: 45-55 minutes
# Exercise Sets x Reps Bell Notes Demo
1 Kettlebell Swings 5 x 20 Heavy Longer sets than Monday. Push through. Sustained output, not peak power. Watch Demo
2 High Pulls 5 x 20 Medium Sustained effort. Keep form tight even as fatigue builds. Watch Demo
3 Side (Golf) Swings 4 x 25 Medium Standard sets but minimal rest. Keep moving. Watch Demo
4 Rows 4 x 25 Heavy 12-13 per arm per set. Faster tempo but still controlled on the lowering. Watch Demo
5 Push Outs 4 x 25 Medium Quick press, controlled return. Breathe rhythmically. Form Cues
6 Overhead Press 4 x 25 Medium Push through the burn. Shoulders will be fatigued — that's the point. Watch Demo
7 Low Chest Flys 4 x 25 Medium Steady rhythm. Don't let form collapse. Form Cues
8 Bicep Curls + Press 4 x 25 Light-Med Fluid continuous motion. No pausing between curl and press. Form Cues
9 Tricep Extensions 4 x 25 Light Drop to light bell if needed. Volume + speed over weight today. Form Cues
10 Side Extensions 4 x 25 Medium Controlled lean, don't jerk. Watch Demo
11 Waist Halos 2 x 50 Light Two long sets instead of four. Keep moving. Watch Demo
12 Head Halos 2 x 50 Light Two long continuous sets. Finish strong. Form Cues
Total: 1,200 reps — Faster pace = higher calorie burn, greater cardiovascular demand

Cool-Down (5 Minutes)

  • Walk slowly for 2 minutes
  • Lat stretch (overhead reach and lean): 30 sec per side
  • Figure-4 glute stretch: 30 sec per side
  • Tricep/shoulder stretch: 30 sec per side
