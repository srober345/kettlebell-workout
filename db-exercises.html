Dumbbell Exercise Library

14 exercises selected from EMG research — form cues, illustrations & tutorials

1

Bulgarian Split Squat

Compound Lower

Rear foot elevated on bench — lower until rear knee nearly touches floor

Watch Tutorial

Form Cues

  • Stand 2 feet in front of bench, place rear foot laces-down on bench
  • Hold dumbbells at sides, shoulders back and down
  • Lower straight down — front shin stays vertical
  • Rear knee drops toward floor, not forward
  • Drive through front heel to stand, squeeze glute at top
  • Keep torso upright — slight forward lean is OK
  • If balance is an issue, use lighter weights until stable
Used in: Strength (M) 4x6/leg — Metabolic (F) via Goblet Squat variation
2

DB Romanian Deadlift

Compound Lower

Hinge at hips, slide DBs down thighs — stop when you feel hamstring stretch

Watch Tutorial

Form Cues

  • Stand with feet hip-width, soft bend in knees (10-15 degrees)
  • Push hips BACK, not down — this is a hinge, not a squat
  • DBs slide along the front of your thighs, staying close to body
  • Lower until you feel a strong hamstring stretch (usually mid-shin)
  • Keep back flat — neutral spine throughout
  • Drive hips forward to stand, squeeze glutes at top
  • Think "close the car door with your hips"
Used in: Strength (M) 4x8 — Hypertrophy (W) SS1b 3x12 — Metabolic (F) Primer 3x12
3

Reverse Lunge

Compound Lower

Step backward, not forward — less knee shear, more glute activation

Watch Tutorial

Form Cues

  • Stand tall with DBs at sides, step one foot BACKWARD
  • Lower until rear knee nearly touches floor (both knees at ~90 degrees)
  • Front shin stays vertical — knee doesn't shoot past toes
  • Drive through front heel to return to start
  • Alternate legs or complete all reps on one side, then switch
  • Keep torso upright throughout — core braced
Used in: Hypertrophy (W) 3x10/leg — Metabolic (F) Circuit A 10/leg
4

Incline DB Press (30°)

Compound Push

Bench at 30° incline — 91% MVC for upper chest activation

Watch Tutorial

Form Cues

  • Set bench to 30 degrees (one or two notches from flat)
  • Feet flat on floor, shoulder blades pinched back and down
  • Start with DBs at chest height, palms forward
  • Press up and slightly together — don't clang them at top
  • Lower to deep stretch at chest level (deeper than barbell allows)
  • 1-second pause at bottom on strength day
  • Maintain natural arch in lower back — don't flatten
Used in: Strength (M) 4x8 — Metabolic (F) Circuit A 12 reps
5

One-Arm DB Row

Compound Pull

Hand and knee on bench — pull DB to hip, squeeze shoulder blade

Watch Tutorial

Form Cues

  • Place one hand and same-side knee on bench for support
  • Free arm hangs straight down holding DB, back flat like a table
  • Pull DB to your hip (not your armpit) — leads with elbow
  • Squeeze shoulder blade toward spine for 1 second at top
  • Lower under control — don't just drop the weight
  • Avoid rotating torso — hips and shoulders stay square
  • Think "start a lawnmower" motion
Used in: Strength (M) 4x8/arm — Hypertrophy (W) SS2b 3x12/arm — Metabolic (F) Circuit A 10/arm
6

Flat DB Press

Compound Push

Flat bench — 93% MVC mid chest. DBs allow greater ROM than barbell.

Watch Tutorial

Form Cues

  • Lie flat on bench, feet planted, shoulder blades retracted
  • Start with DBs at chest level, palms facing feet
  • Press up to lockout — DBs come slightly together at top
  • Lower slowly to a deep stretch — elbows at 45-degree angle
  • Pause 1 sec at bottom on strength day for maximum tension
  • Keep natural back arch — don't flatten lumbar spine
Used in: Strength (M) 3x8 — Hypertrophy (W) SS2a 3x12
7

Standing Overhead Press

Compound Push

Standing engages core as stabilizer — 79% MVC for shoulders

Watch Tutorial

Form Cues

  • Stand with feet shoulder-width, core braced tight
  • DBs start at shoulder height, palms facing forward
  • Press straight up to full lockout — biceps near ears
  • Don't arch back — squeeze glutes to prevent leaning
  • Lower under control back to shoulders
  • Breathe out on the press, in on the lower
Used in: Strength (M) 3x8 — Hypertrophy (W) SS3b 3x10 — Metabolic (F) via Thruster
8

DB Floor Fly

Accessory

Lying on floor — arc DBs from wide to together. Floor limits dangerous ROM.

Watch Tutorial

Form Cues

  • Lie on floor (not bench) — knees bent, feet flat
  • Start with DBs pressed up above chest
  • Lower in a wide arc until upper arms touch the floor
  • Floor provides natural ROM limiter — protects shoulders
  • Squeeze chest to bring DBs back together in an arc
  • Keep slight bend in elbows throughout — never lock arms
  • Focus on the squeeze at the top
Used in: Hypertrophy (W) SS3a 3x12 — Metabolic (F) Circuit B 12 reps
9

Incline Curl

Isolation

Incline angle stretches long head — higher activation than standing curl

Watch Tutorial

Form Cues

  • Set bench to 45-55 degrees, sit back fully
  • Let arms hang straight down — feel the bicep stretch
  • Curl DBs up by bending elbows only — no swinging
  • Squeeze biceps hard at the top for 1 second
  • Lower slowly (3 sec on strength day) — this is where growth happens
  • Keep upper arms stationary — don't let elbows drift forward
Used in: Strength (M) 3x8 — Metabolic (F) Circuit A 10 reps
10

Hammer Curl

Isolation

Neutral (thumbs-up) grip targets brachialis for complete arm development

Watch Tutorial

Form Cues

  • Stand tall, DBs at sides with neutral grip (palms face each other)
  • Curl both DBs up simultaneously, keeping palms facing in
  • Don't rotate wrists — thumbs point up the entire time
  • Squeeze at the top, lower slowly (2-3 seconds)
  • Keep elbows pinned to your sides — no swinging
  • Can alternate arms if preferred
Used in: Hypertrophy (W) SS4a 3x12
11

Overhead Tricep Extension

Isolation

One DB, both hands — lower behind head, extend to lockout

Watch Tutorial

Form Cues

  • Hold one DB with both hands (diamond grip on the top plate)
  • Start with DB overhead, arms fully extended
  • Lower behind head by bending elbows only (3-sec negative)
  • Keep elbows pointing forward — don't let them flare wide
  • Feel full tricep stretch at bottom, then extend back to lockout
  • Upper arms stay stationary — only forearms move
Used in: Strength (M) 3x10 — Metabolic (F) Circuit B 12 reps
12

DB Skull Crusher

Isolation

Lying on bench — lower DBs to temples, extend to lockout

Watch Tutorial

Form Cues

  • Lie flat on bench, DBs pressed up with neutral grip (palms face each other)
  • Keep upper arms vertical — only bend at the elbows
  • Lower DBs slowly (3 sec) toward your temples/forehead
  • Don't actually touch your skull — stop at ear level
  • Extend back to lockout, squeezing triceps at top
  • Elbows stay fixed — don't let them drift outward
Used in: Hypertrophy (W) SS4b 3x12
13

Lateral Raise

Accessory

Raise to shoulder height only — peak medial deltoid activation

Watch Tutorial

Form Cues

  • Stand with slight forward lean (5-10 degrees)
  • DBs at sides, slight bend in elbows (locked throughout)
  • Raise arms out to sides, leading with elbows not hands
  • Stop at shoulder height — going higher risks impingement
  • Pause 1 second at top, lower under control (2 sec)
  • Think "pour water from a pitcher" at the top
  • Don't use momentum — if you're swinging, go lighter
Used in: Strength (M) SS 3x10 — Hypertrophy (W) SS5a 3x12 — Metabolic (F) Circuit B 12 reps
14

Bent-Over Rear Delt Fly

Accessory

85% MVC posterior deltoid — critical for shoulder balance and posture

Watch Tutorial

Form Cues

  • Hinge forward at hips to 45 degrees, flat back
  • DBs hang straight down, slight bend in elbows
  • Raise arms out to sides in a reverse fly motion
  • Squeeze shoulder blades together at top for 1 second
  • Lower under control — don't just drop the weights
  • Keep chest pointing at floor throughout
  • Think "spread your wings"
Used in: Strength (M) SS 3x10 — Hypertrophy (W) SS5b 3x12
