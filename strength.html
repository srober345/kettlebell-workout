Strength Day

Strength Workout

Goal: Neural adaptation — maximize force production per rep
Rest: 90-120 sec between sets
Tempo: 3-1-1 (3s down, 1s pause, 1s up)
Time: ~55 minutes
# Exercise Sets x Reps Weight Rest Notes Demo
1 Bulgarian Split Squat 4 x 6/leg 25-30 lb 120s Rear foot elevated on bench. 3-sec lowering, 1-sec pause at bottom. Drive through front heel. Form Cues
2 DB Romanian Deadlift 4 x 8 25-30 lb 90s Hinge at hips, soft knee bend. DBs slide along thighs. Feel hamstring stretch before driving up. Form Cues
3 Incline DB Press (30°) 4 x 8 25-30 lb 90s Bench at 30°. Lower to chest level, 1-sec pause, press to lockout. Squeeze chest at top. Form Cues
4 One-Arm DB Row 4 x 8/arm 25-30 lb 90s Hand and knee on bench. Pull to hip, squeeze shoulder blade 1 sec. Control the negative. Form Cues
5 Flat DB Press 3 x 8 25-30 lb 90s Flat bench. Lower to chest stretch, 1-sec pause, press up. Full ROM — deeper than barbell allows. Form Cues
6 Standing Overhead Press 3 x 8 20-25 lb 90s Start at shoulders, press straight up. Core braced — no arching. Full lockout at top. Form Cues
7 Incline Curl 3 x 8 15-20 lb 75s Bench at 45°. Arms hang straight down. Curl without swinging. 3-sec negative. Form Cues
8 Overhead Tricep Extension 3 x 10 15-20 lb 75s One DB, both hands. Lower behind head slowly (3 sec). Elbows point forward, don't flare. Form Cues
9a SSLateral Raise 3 x 10 10-15 lb — Slight forward lean. Raise to shoulder height. Lead with elbows, not hands. Form Cues
9b SSBent-Over Rear Delt Fly 3 x 10 10-15 lb 60s Hinged at 45°. Squeeze shoulder blades at top. Control the descent. Form Cues
10 Farmer's Walk (40 sec) 3 x 1 25-30 lb 60s Walk with purpose. Shoulders back and down, core braced. Grip hard. 40 seconds per walk. Form Cues
Total: ~300 reps — 37 working sets — Heaviest dumbbells available
