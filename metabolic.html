Metabolic Day

Metabolic Workout

Goal: Maximum EPOC — burn calories for up to 38 hours post-workout
Rest: 30-45 sec between circuits
Tempo: 2-0-1 (controlled, fluid)
Time: ~45 minutes

Compound primer first, then two circuits of 3 rounds each, finishing with push-ups and farmer's walk.

Block 1 — Compound Primer (Straight Sets, 45s Rest)
# Exercise Sets x Reps Weight Rest Notes Demo
1 DB Thruster 3 x 12 20-25 lb 45s Squat to press in one fluid motion. Full depth squat, explosive press to lockout. Form Cues
2 DB Romanian Deadlift 3 x 12 20-25 lb 45s Controlled hinge. Keep moving — don't rest at top. Maintain tension throughout. Form Cues
Block 2 — Circuit A (3 Rounds, 30s Rest Between Rounds)
# Exercise Reps Weight Notes Demo
3a Reverse Lunge 10/leg 15-20 lb Alternating legs. Keep tempo steady. No pausing between legs. Form Cues
3b Incline DB Press 12 15-20 lb Lighter than strength day. Keep rhythm — 2 sec down, 1 sec up. Form Cues
3c One-Arm Row 10/arm 15-20 lb No bench needed — hinge forward, free hand on knee. Move quickly between arms. Form Cues
3d Incline Curl 10 10-15 lb Controlled tempo. No swinging. Then rest 30 sec before starting next round. Form Cues
Block 3 — Circuit B (3 Rounds, 30s Rest Between Rounds)
# Exercise Reps Weight Notes Demo
4a Goblet Squat 15 20-25 lb One DB at chest. Full depth. Keep moving — 15 straight reps, no pausing. Form Cues
4b DB Floor Fly 12 15-20 lb Lying on floor. Slow fly motion. Squeeze chest hard at top. Form Cues
4c Overhead Tricep Extension 12 10-15 lb One DB, both hands. Keep elbows tight. Full ROM. Form Cues
4d Lateral Raise 12 10-15 lb Last exercise in circuit. Controlled — don't let form break. Then rest 30 sec. Form Cues
Block 4 — Finisher
# Exercise Sets x Reps Weight Rest Notes
5 Push-Ups 2 x max Bodyweight 30s Full ROM push-ups to failure. Chest to floor. If you can do 20+, elevate feet on bench.
6 Farmer's Walk (45 sec) 3 x 1 25-30 lb 30s Heaviest DBs available. Walk with purpose, shoulders packed. 45 seconds per walk.
Total: ~400 reps — 27 working sets — 2 circuits of 3 rounds + finisher
